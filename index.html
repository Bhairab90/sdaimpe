About Us

Welcome to SDA Impex!

SDA Impex aims to create an ideal integrated flooring solution. Our team follows a simple philosophy of quality blended with innovation. Starting from concept to execution, our specialized team works along with you to deliver your particular requirements according to the budget and time period. Our wide array of product categories comprises of flooring for gym, football turf, children’s playground, table tennis court, cricket turf, basketball court, tennis court, golf turf, deck wood, rubber flooring, and so on. These are available in a number of alternative shades, colours, designs, patterns, quality standards, price range, etc. to provide customers with a wide range of options to choose from.
